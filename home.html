About Us

Motosports club is the technical outreach club of NIT RAIPUR. the club promotes innovative thinking and the technical skills of the members. It gives a complete theoretical and practical outreach required in the automotive field.

This club spark a spark of passion for automotive in young minds and try to engage and educate everyone with the events. The club also has active participation in all the major competitions.

The club's main aim is to train young mindsets in the technical field and nurture them with skills beneficial for their future.

Our Events

FORMULA BHARAT

Formula Bharat is an engineering design competition in which students from colleges and universities all over the country compete with a life-size Formula-style vehicle in areas of engineering design, overall cost, marketability, and dynamic performance.

eBAJA

eBAJA is an electric engine driven BAJA event organised by SAEINDIA. This was a new initiative introduced in 2014 on the counsel of former President of India, APJ Abdul Kalam, in order to promote greener mobility.
